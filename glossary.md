# Trauma-Informed Bot Glossary

### Technical Terms
* **API:** The bridge between our bot and TrackHS/EHR.
* **Sentiment Analysis:** Detecting if a guest is "Happy" or "Stressed."
* **F-String:** A way to make guest messages dynamic.

### Industry Terms
* **STR:** Short-Term Rental industry.
* **EHR:** Electronic Health Record (for our medical bots).
* **Tape Chart:** The visual calendar in TrackHS.
* * **Float:** A number with a decimal point (e.g., 8.5). In our bots, we use floats to track precise data like "Average Call Time" or "Stress Sentiment Scores."
* * **Markdown** "A lightweight language used to format text"
